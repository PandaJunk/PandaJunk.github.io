## Research Interests
My current research interests focus on **Time Series Analysis** and **LLM Agents**, with an emphasis on:
- *Time Series Forecasting*: non-stationary modeling, frequency-domain methods
- *Time Series Reasoning*: multimodal LLMs for time series understanding and QA
- *LLM Agents*: reasoning, chain-of-thought, and reinforcement learning for complex tasks
